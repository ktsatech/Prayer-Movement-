Opening the Portals of Worship

On June 7th, believers from across the globe are uniting with a single, heart‑felt purpose: to fill the earth with one hour of dedicated praise, worship, and intercession for our nations.

Our Vision

This initiative finds its roots in a divine calling received early this year to "open the portal of worship." We believe that when the Church gathers to lift up the name of Jesus, we shift the spiritual atmosphere of our lands. Aligning with the March for Jesus movement celebrated in various countries, we are calling on fellowships, churches, and small groups everywhere to set aside time on June 7th to exalt God and seek God on behalf of our Nations.

How to Participate

The Format: Each church, fellowship, or community group decides on their own location and time on that very day of 7th June 2026.
The Atmosphere: While indoor gatherings are welcome, we encourage groups to hold their sessions outdoors—taking worship into the public sphere to be a witness to our nations and this also gives an open chance even to those who don't go to church to be part of praying for our Country Uganda.
The Goal: At least a single hour of worshiping, praising and interceding on behalf of Uganda.
Share the Moment: Please record your worship and prayer sessions. Sharing these recordings allows us to gather a global testimony of how God is moving from country to country. You can share the videos by tapping the WhatsApp Icon.

Our Heart for the Nations

As we gather, we lift up these core pillars on behalf of our country Uganda:

Leadership & Government
Praying for wisdom, integrity, and the fear of the Lord in our halls of power.
Unity & Security
Asking for peace, harmony across cultures and religions, and protection from unrest.
Economy & Provision
Interceding for restored economies, job creation, and the well‑being of the workforce.
Safety & Protection
Praying for protection from natural disasters and wisdom for relief efforts.
Public Health
Asking for healing, prevention of outbreaks, and strength for medical workers.
The Next Generation
Standing for the protection of our youth and the rise of a God‑fearing generation.
The Church
Praying for spiritual revival, unity, and that the Church may be the light of the world.
Justice & Order
Asking for fairness in law enforcement and an end to crime and exploitation.
Families
Praying for the restoration, health, and stability of homes everywhere.
Thanksgiving
Ending in gratitude for the unique blessings, beauty, and diversity of our nations.

Join us this June 7th. Let us stand in the gap, lift our voices, and saturate Uganda with the presence of God.
